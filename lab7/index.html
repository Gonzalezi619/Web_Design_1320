Vert Skating

Think Tony Hawk, Humonugs air and half pipes.This type of skating was started back in the 1960's in Los Angeles California.

This was the first real style of skateboarding, some would say. It started with surfers trying to continue their trade in the offseason. Vert skating was similar to surfing with the action of carving the sides of the pool walls.
