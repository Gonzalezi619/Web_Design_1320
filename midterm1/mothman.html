The Mothman

Every State has their mythical creature. In West Virginia it's known as the Moth Man. Half Moth Half Man, it tends to reside in the woods.

Point Pleasent, West Virginia is where the creature has been reportedley sighted.

The book The Mothman Prophecies was published in 1975 and brought the Mothman to the world. It was turned into a movie in 2002.

The city of Point Pleasent holds an annual festiavl to commerate this strange and eerie creature.
